About Chronos

Chronos is a web application designed to help visualize historical events graphically. Historical events are interrelated. They are brought upon most often by people, and in turn they impact people's lives. When studying both, it's hard to understand connected or concurrent events by looking up dates and trying to put them together in your head. Chronos is meant to assist in our understanding of historical events, by providing a visual representation along a timeline. Filters can be applied by using date ranges, people's names, and a variety of tags.
